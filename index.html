Our Services

Complete Protection for Every Surface

From invisible paint shields to head-turning wraps - we handle everything so your car stays flawless in Dubai's toughest conditions.

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Self-healing, invisible armor that shields your paint from rock chips, scratches, and UV damage. Your car stays showroom-new for years - not months.

Window Tinting

Premium thermal tint that blocks Dubai's brutal heat and UV rays. Drive cooler, protect your interior, and enjoy total privacy - without sacrificing visibility.

Vinyl Wrapping

Transform your car's look without repainting. Matte, gloss, satin, chrome - choose your finish and turn heads everywhere from JBR to Sheikh Zayed Road.

Ceramic Coating

A permanent hydrophobic layer that repels water, dirt, and contaminants. One application and your car practically cleans itself - glossy and protected 24/7.

Body Kit Installation & Custom Work

Professional body kit fitting, caliper painting, detailing, and custom modifications. Whatever your vision - we bring it to life with dealer-level precision that makes your car look factory-fresh.
